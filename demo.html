Mathematics



The Department offers B.Sc. (Hons.) Mathematics to students of all streams (Humanities, Commerce and Science) with different optional (credit and qualifying) subjects for each stream. The department also offers Mathematics as a discipline course in B.A. Programme, concurrent course for B.A. (H) and main subject in B.Sc. (Hons.) Chemistry, B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry, B.Sc. (Hons.) Botany, B.Sc. (Hons.) Zoology and B.Sc. Life Sciences. Besides enhancing their logical ability skills, students enrolling for these courses have various possible avenues that lead them to either further post-graduation degree in Mathematics, MBA and MCA or careers in Teaching, Insurance and Banking etc.
